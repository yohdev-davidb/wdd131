Getting Started

Welcome to D&D Character Forge

Creating your first character is easy! Follow these steps to begin your adventure:

  1. Click the "Character Creator" button in the navigation
  2. Fill out your character's basic information
  3. Set your ability scores
  4. Add character details and personality
  5. Save and export your character sheet

Basic Information

Character Basics

The first step is filling out your character's basic information:

  • Name: Choose a name that fits your character's race and background
  • Race: Select from available D&D races, each with unique traits
  • Class: Choose your character's primary class
  • Level: Starting characters typically begin at level 1
  • Alignment: Choose your character's moral and ethical stance

Ability Scores

Understanding Abilities

Your character's abilities represent their basic attributes:

Strength

Physical power and carrying capacity

Dexterity

Agility, reflexes, and balance

Constitution

Endurance and stamina

Intelligence

Memory and reasoning

Wisdom

Perception and insight

Charisma

Force of personality

Character Details

Personality and Background

Bring your character to life with detailed descriptions:

  • Background Story: Your character's history and origins
  • Personality Traits: Distinctive behaviors and attitudes
  • Ideals: Principles and beliefs that drive your character
  • Bonds: Connections to people, places, or causes
  • Flaws: Character weaknesses and imperfections

Yes! You can return to the character creator at any time to edit your saved characters.
